Does EverClean show how many professionals are available by ZIP code?

EverClean should only display professional availability based on real, active, verified, service-eligible professionals in that market. The platform should avoid inflated or generic counts that do not reflect actual coverage.

FAQs

Questions customers ask before booking

These answers support conversion while avoiding claims that should only be made after operational verification.

Do I see the final price before booking?

Yes. EverClean calculates and shows the final quote inside the app before the customer confirms. Public pages do not display pricing.

Does EverClean require a membership?

No required membership is advertised on the website. Optional programs or rewards should be clearly presented inside the app if enabled.

Are professionals background checked?

EverClean is structured to support professional onboarding and verification. Any public background-check claim must match the actual onboarding process in production.

Can I choose my cleaner?

The marketplace can support cleaner profiles and rebooking flows. If cleaner selection is enabled in the app, it should be presented clearly at booking.

Can I rebook the same professional?

This should be enabled as a marketplace feature when operationally available. Until then, use careful language such as “where available.”

Do professionals bring supplies?

Supply expectations should be stated inside the booking flow. Customers may be able to add notes if they prefer specific products or equipment.

What if something is damaged or missing?

EverClean may help review eligible concerns for services booked and paid through the platform. Resolution is subject to review, platform rules, documentation, and applicable insurance or bonding terms.

What if I need to reschedule?

Rescheduling rules should be displayed inside the app before confirmation and should be consistent with EverClean’s published terms.

Do you clean offices, clinics, or commercial spaces?

Yes. EverClean has service pages for office cleaning, post-construction cleaning, and medical / clinical cleaning. Commercial clients should request a custom quote or walkthrough.

How are reviews handled?

Only verified customer reviews from completed bookings should be published. Do not use fabricated reviews or testimonials.

Legal readiness

Public language that protects EverClean

These blocks should be reviewed by counsel before production.

Reviews and testimonials

Publish only verified reviews from real customers after completed bookings. Do not publish invented reviews as if they are real.

Insurance and COI

Public pages should say EverClean maintains general liability insurance for its business operations, subject to policy terms, limits, exclusions, and eligibility. Do not publish policy number, dates, or limits on the website.

Theft and damage

Use review-and-mediation language unless a specific insurance or bond confirms reimbursement.

Off-platform services

Trust, safety, payment support, and claim-review processes should apply only to eligible services booked and paid through EverClean.
