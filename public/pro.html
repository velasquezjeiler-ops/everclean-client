Privacy and verification

Built to avoid storing SSN in EverClean’s marketplace database

EverClean does not collect or store Social Security Numbers directly in its marketplace database. Identity, payout, tax, and applicable verification steps are handled through trusted third-party providers such as Stripe and approved verification partners.

Stripe Connect payout setup

Stripe may collect identity, tax, banking, and payout eligibility information. EverClean stores only account status and reference fields needed to manage platform access.

Provider FAQ

Common questions for EverClean Pros

Does EverClean collect my SSN?

No. EverClean does not collect or store Social Security Numbers directly in its marketplace database. Identity, payout, tax, and applicable verification steps are handled through trusted third-party providers.

Are jobs guaranteed?

No. Access to opportunities depends on customer demand, location, service category, provider availability, platform approval, and account status.

Can one Pro support multiple services?

Yes. One profile can include multiple service capabilities. Some categories may require additional review, equipment, documents, or verification.

Can companies join?

Yes. Individuals, teams, and provider businesses can begin onboarding. Business accounts may require additional documentation, insurance, or compliance review.
