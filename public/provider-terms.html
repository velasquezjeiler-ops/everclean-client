No Guaranteed Jobs or Income

EverClean does not guarantee job volume, income, schedules, territories, customer demand or acceptance into any service category.

Independent Service Providers

EverClean works with independent service providers and provider businesses, subject to the final agreement and applicable law.

Service Capabilities

One provider profile may include multiple service capabilities, which can be approved, held, limited or reviewed based on experience, documentation and platform needs.

Platform Access

Access to the Pro dashboard and opportunities depends on account status, onboarding, verification, payout setup, compliance, and platform approval.

Payouts

Payout setup is handled through Stripe Connect. Payout timing and eligibility are subject to platform rules, Stripe status, disputes and payment availability.

Suspension / Removal

EverClean may suspend or limit platform access for safety, fraud, policy violations, customer disputes, failed verification or account-risk concerns.
