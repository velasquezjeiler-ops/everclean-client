Integrated Pro Capability Network

One EverClean Pro profile can qualify for multiple specialized service capabilities.

EverClean Pros are integrated multi-service professionals. During onboarding, each provider selects the capabilities they can perform, and the platform can approve categories such as residential, Airbnb turnover, commercial, janitorial, post-construction, clinical, and specialized cleaning based on experience, equipment, service area, availability, and applicable verification.

Residential Capability Track

House cleaning, standard cleaning, deep cleaning, same-day options, and move-in / move-out capabilities for homes, apartments, and condos.

Airbnb Turnover Track

Guest-ready short-term rental resets with linen, bathroom, kitchen, entry, restock, and photo-proof workflows where available.

Commercial Capability Track

Office, coworking, retail, gym, and recurring business cleaning capabilities handled through business-ready service scopes.

Clinical / Wellness Track

Medical, dental, therapy, wellness, and patient-facing spaces with service expectations built around presentation and high-touch areas, where eligible.

Post-Construction Track

Post-construction and renovation cleaning capabilities for dust, glass, fixtures, floors, built-ins, and final handoff preparation.

Specialized Cleaning Track

Add-on and specialty capabilities such as interior windows, appliances, cabinets, baseboards, restock, inspection, and photo checklist support.

Capability approval rule: EverClean should present Pros as integrated multi-service providers, not as isolated single-service cleaners. Public pages may describe capability tracks, but job access remains based on approved categories, service area, account status, demand, and platform review.
