EverClean Status Fields

EverClean stores status/reference data such as stripe_account_id, onboarding_status, charges_enabled, payouts_enabled and requirements_due.

No Direct Bank / SSN Storage

EverClean does not store full bank account numbers, SSN, full tax IDs or raw Stripe identity documents in its marketplace database.

Payout Eligibility

Eligible payouts depend on Stripe account status, platform rules, completed service requirements, disputes, holds, customer payment status and applicable law.
