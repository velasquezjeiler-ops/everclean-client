Third-Party Verification Disclosure

Public-safe disclosure structure for identity and applicable verification handled outside EverClean’s marketplace database.

Draft template only. This page provides the structure and business intent for Claude/Replit. Final enforceable language must be reviewed by counsel before production.

Third-Party Provider

Applicable verification steps may be handled by approved third-party providers depending on service category, location, platform requirements and account status.

No SSN Storage in Marketplace DB

EverClean does not collect or store Social Security Numbers directly in its marketplace database. Sensitive identity/verification data is handled by trusted third-party providers.

Status-Only Model

EverClean receives only status and reference information needed to manage platform access, such as pending, clear, review required, failed, expired or manual review.

FCRA / Fair Chance Handling

If a consumer report is used, disclosure, authorization, pre-adverse and adverse action workflows must be implemented through counsel/vendor-approved forms.
