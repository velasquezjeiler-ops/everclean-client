One app. Multiple cleaning professionals. Every type of space.

EverClean is not positioned as another home-cleaning-only company. It is built as a multi-pro marketplace for residential, short-term rental, commercial, clinical, construction, and specialized cleaning needs.

Residential Capability Track

Homes, apartments, condos, recurring cleaning, deep cleaning, same-day options, and move cleaning capability.

Airbnb Turnover Track

Short-term rentals, Airbnb turnovers, guest-ready resets, restock, and photo-proof workflows.

Business Pros

Offices, coworking spaces, retail spaces, gyms, recurring contracts, and commercial quote workflows.

Clinical / Wellness Track

Medical clinics, dental offices, therapy offices, wellness centers, and patient-facing environments.

Project Pros

Post-construction, renovation cleanup, final handoff cleaning, dust removal, floors, fixtures, and built-ins.

Specialized Cleaning Track

Windows, appliances, cabinets, baseboards, photo checklists, inventory support, and quality inspection.

App modules needed

What the app must support to make multi-pro real

The website can explain the model, but the app must prove it with operational workflows.

Verified availability

Address and ZIP coverage should confirm actual service categories available in each market.

Professional categories

Professionals should be approved by service type, not shown as generic cleaners for every job.

Photo checklist

Proof-of-work by service type: residential, turnover, office, clinical, and construction.

Verified reviews

Reviews should be connected to completed bookings, service categories, and professionals.

Issue reporting

Damage, access, scope, and condition issues should be documented with photos and status tracking.

Professional dashboard

Pros need service eligibility, job offers, checklists, earnings, ratings, documents, and badges.
